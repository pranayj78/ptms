# PTMS – Personal Tax Management System

## Vision

PTMS is an enterprise-grade personal tax management platform designed for Indian taxpayers who own domestic and foreign financial assets.

The system aims to automate tax computation, investment tracking, reconciliation, and statutory reporting while maintaining complete auditability.

Unlike traditional spreadsheets, PTMS separates data, business rules, and presentation into independent layers.

## Goals

- Single source of truth for all tax data
- Complete audit trail
- Zero manual FIFO calculation
- Automated Schedule FA generation
- Automated Capital Gain computation
- Old/New Tax Regime comparison
- Reusable across financial years
- Extensible through plugins

## Non-Goals

PTMS is not intended to replace the Income Tax Portal.

Instead it generates verified working papers supporting ITR filing.
